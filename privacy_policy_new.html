Privacy Policy for Fashho

Last updated: February 2026

Fashho ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Fashho mobile application.

Overview

The Fashho mobile application is a simple product showcase application. It displays a limited number of fashion product images and allows users to visit our official website to view more details or make purchases.

Information Collected by the App

The Fashho mobile application itself does NOT collect, store, or process any personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Payment information
  • Location data

Users can browse product images in the app without providing any personal data.

External Website Redirection

When a user taps on a product inside the Fashho app, the app redirects the user to our official website using the device's web browser.

On the website, users may be required to provide personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Shipping address
  • Payment information

This information is collected only on the website and is governed by the website's own privacy policy.

Third-Party Services

The app may use standard Android and Google Play Services which may automatically collect certain non-personal information such as device information or crash logs for system functionality.

For more information, please review Google's privacy policy.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to ensure users are safely redirected to our official website for transactions.

Children’s Privacy

The Fashho app is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
